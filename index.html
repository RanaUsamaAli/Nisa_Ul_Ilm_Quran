About Us

WHO ARE WE?

Nisa-ul-Ilm-Quran is a global, online Quran Academy with offices in Rawalpindi/ Islamabad, Pakistan. Founded in 2020 post Covid, we have been fulfilling the Sunnah of the Prophet PBUH of teaching the Quran to our fellow Muslim brothers, sisters & their children since. Currently, we are offering our services to residents of Gulf and European countries as well as US and Canada.

OUR VISION

  • VISION

    We aim to provide a platform anyone can use to dive deeper into Quranic Studies. Anyone looking to learn more about the book of Allah will be able to benefit from it, building a strong relationship with Allah and securing their future in the Afterlife.

    • OUR MISSION

  • MISSION

    Our mission is to bring the people closer to Allah. The first step in this journey is to teach the Quran to Muslims around the world. In our experience, people have the desire to gain knowledge of the Quran, but unfortunately, most live far away from well-known Islamic Institutions, making it difficult to travel in search of knowledge of the Quran. Therefore, we at Nisa-ul-Ilm, bring that knowledge to you, wherever you may be. You can now learn the Quran from the comfort of your own home.

 

Services

Online Makharij & Ilm At-Tajweed Course

Makharij means "the place of articulation (in the mouth)." Tajweed means "to enhance or to make excellent." In this course, you will learn how to properly say each letter of the Arabic alphabet. (Makharij.) You will also learn the proper rules of each letter of the Arabic alphabet to give them their due rights during recitation. (Tajweed.)


For Kids and Adults.

Online Holy Quran Recitation Course

Learn to recite the Holy Quran with accurate pronounciation and precise origins.


For Kids and Adults.

Online Holy Quran Hifz Program

The word "Hafiz" means protector. Therefore, someone who memorizes the entire Quran is the protector of the Book of Allah SWT. What an honor!


In this program, we offer the following:
- Hifz of Last Juz
- Hifz of Small Surahs
- Hifz of Chosen Surahs

- Hifz of the Entire Quran

(Ijaza)


For Kids and Adults.

Online Prayer Etiquette Course

Prayer is one of the Five Pillars of Islam. Therefore, it is important that you know the etiquettes of prayer so that you can perform it accurately. In this course, you will learn all you need about the proper etiquettes of prayer. In addition, you will learn, Eid, Funeral, and Nawafil prayers.


For Kids and Adults.

Online Masnoon Duas Course

In this course, you will learn all the Duas our Prophet PBUH taught us to recite for every occasion throughout our daily lives.


For Kids and Adults.
